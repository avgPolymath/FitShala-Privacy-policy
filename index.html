Privacy Policy for Fitshala AI

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Fitshala AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Phone Number: Used exclusively for authentication (OTP Login) via Supabase.
  • Name (Optional): Used to personalize your experience.

B. Health & Fitness Data

To provide personalized workout plans, we collect:

  • Physical Goals: (e.g., Muscle Gain, Fat Loss).
  • Experience Level: (e.g., Novice, Returner).
  • Pain Map: Specific body areas you flag as painful (e.g., "Knee Pain") to adjust your exercises.
  • Workout Logs: Exercises performed, weights lifted, sets, and reps.

C. Device & Usage Data

  • Device Information: We may collect device model and OS version to optimize performance.
  • Analytics: We use anonymous analytics to understand app usage patterns (e.g., "Workout Completed").

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To generate and adjust your weekly workout plans (the "AI Coach" feature).
  • Account Management: To verify your identity and restore your data if you switch devices.
  • Improvement: To analyze aggregate data and improve our progression algorithms.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Storage: Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS).
  • Third Parties: We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted third-party services:
    • Supabase: For authentication and database hosting.
    • ImageKit: For delivering exercise media.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information where required by law.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion at any time within the App settings or by contacting fitshala.ai@gmail.com. Upon deletion, all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (Row Level Security, HTTPS encryption) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Fitshala AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
